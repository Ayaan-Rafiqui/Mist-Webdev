Kapu Beach, Udupi

About Kapu Beach

Kapu Beach (also spelled Kaup Beach) is one of the most beautiful coastal destinations in Udupi, Karnataka. Famous for its iconic lighthouse, golden sands, and tranquil sunsets, Kapu offers the perfect mix of natural beauty and peaceful charm. The beach is less crowded, making it a great spot for evening walks, photography, and relaxation.

How to Reach

Kapu Beach is about 13 km from Udupi city and 45 km from Mangalore. You can reach it easily by local buses, taxis, or private vehicles. The nearest railway station is Udupi, and Mangalore International Airport is the closest airport.
